Applications 21 to 30 July 2017

Exciting that you are thinking of attending 10 Days of Code in Rotterdam!

Our application process is simple: Follow the link below to sign up for our amazing ten day event before the 31st of May. We will then review your application and schedule an interview with you early in June. You will be notified by June 10th (latest) of your application status.

If you have zero coding experience, no problem! We are looking for inspiring and motivated people like yourself to bring a breath of fresh air to the IT industry. You will, however, be required to do 10 hours of pre-work before the event. This really helps you to figure out if coding is really something for you in advance :)

Please note that the program is full time . In very rare cases we have made exceptions for students to miss one day of the event. The reason for this is that you will be learning (amongst others) from your peers, and they from you. This is not possible if you are not there. So if you're ready...
