10 Days of Code, Rotterdam, 15-24th July 2016

What

10 Days of Code 2016 is a free coding event held in Rotterdam city center. During the event we will provide you with a place to set up (comfortable indoor) camp, a mentor, free food, coffee and internet - all the things you need to build something great!

Why

Attending the event will give you the opportunity to learn something new and build something great. You will meet other amazing and inspiring programmers. Who knows, you might even finish your pet project that's been on the shelf for the past couple of years ;) Space is limited, so sign up now!

Who You Are

Anyone can attend. Whether you are a complete beginner who would like to learn coding from scratch or an expert who would like to launch an exciting new project, everyone is welcome. We believe that a diverse group of participants will foster a creative and inspiring learning atmosphere. We are looking for people with a lot of enthusiasm and dedication who are willing to try something new.

Applications

We will accept an equal amount of men and women to the event. All applicants will have a short virtual interview before being allowed to attend.

Who We Are

Days of Code is a foundation dedicated to providing free coding education. Our events are focused around self-directed learning. We believe that if we provide an inspiring and nurturing environment, you will be able to thrive. All funding is arranged through donations. We believe that education wants to be free.
